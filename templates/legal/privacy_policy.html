Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

EchoDesk ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information when you use our Facebook integration:

  • Facebook Page Information: Page name, Page ID, and basic page metadata
  • Messages: Messages sent to your Facebook pages by customers
  • User Account Information: Your name, email, and basic profile information needed to manage your account

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display customer messages from your Facebook pages in the EchoDesk dashboard
  • Provide customer service management and ticketing functionality
  • Send notifications about new messages and system updates
  • Improve our service and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data

7. Facebook Integration

When you connect your Facebook pages to EchoDesk:

  • We only access pages you explicitly authorize
  • We only read messages sent to your pages (we do not access your personal messages)
  • You can revoke access at any time through Facebook or EchoDesk settings
  • We comply with Facebook's Platform Policy and Data Use Policy

8. Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data, you can:

  • Use our data deletion request form
  • Contact us directly at support@echodesk.ge
  • Delete your account through the EchoDesk dashboard

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
